Booking FAQs

Common questions about our booking process

How far in advance should I book?
We recommend booking at least 1-2 weeks in advance for weekday appointments and 2-3 weeks for weekend appointments. For complex services or specific stylists, booking even earlier is advised.
What is your cancellation policy?
We require 24 hours notice for cancellations to avoid a charge. For appointments canceled with less than 24 hours notice, a 50% fee may be applied. No-shows will be charged the full amount of the service.
Do I need to pay a deposit?
For new clients or appointments over 2 hours, we may require a credit card to secure your booking. The card will not be charged unless the cancellation policy is violated.
What if I'm running late?
Please call us if you're running late. We'll do our best to accommodate you, but if you're more than 15 minutes late, we may need to reschedule your appointment.
