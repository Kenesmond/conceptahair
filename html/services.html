Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services

How often should I get my hair cut?
For maintaining healthy hair and style, we recommend every 6-8 weeks. However, this can vary based on your hair type, length, and style.
How long will my color service last?
Permanent color typically lasts 4-6 weeks before noticeable regrowth. With proper care using our recommended products, your color can stay vibrant for longer.
Do you use organic products?
We use a range of high-quality professional products, including several organic and vegan lines. Let your stylist know your preferences during consultation.
Can I bring a photo for inspiration?
Absolutely! We encourage clients to bring photos to help communicate their desired look. We'll discuss what's achievable for your hair type and lifestyle.
