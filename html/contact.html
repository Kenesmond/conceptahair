Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What are your COVID-19 safety measures?
We follow all recommended health guidelines, including regular sanitization, mask-wearing for staff, and social distancing in the salon. All tools and stations are thoroughly disinfected between clients.
Do I need to book in advance?
While walk-ins are welcome based on availability, we highly recommend booking in advance to secure your preferred time slot, especially for weekends and complex services.
What forms of payment do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, cash, and mobile payment options like Apple Pay and Google Pay.
Do you offer gift certificates?
Yes, we offer gift certificates in any denomination. They can be purchased in-salon or over the phone, and make perfect gifts for any occasion.
What is your cancellation policy?
We require 24 hours notice for cancellations to avoid a charge. For appointments canceled with less than 24 hours notice, a 50% fee may be applied.
